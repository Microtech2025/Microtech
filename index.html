About Micro Tech Center

Micro Tech Center, founded by a Jenny K Paulose, is a premier educational academy offering a wide range of training programs including Computer Courses under CAPT, over 70 government-certified LBS Skill Centre Courses, Gama Abacus coaching, and Fashion Designing programs.

With a commitment to excellence in both education and technology, Micro Tech Center also includes Micro Computers — a full-fledged PC dealership, service center, and custom PC builder, offering everything from lab setup to server building.

About Gama Abacus

Gama Abacus (est. 1999, Thrissur) is one of India’s leading abacus education institutions, offering both online and offline mental arithmetic training, teacher programs, and franchise opportunities. The official headquarters is in Thrissur, Kerala.

MicroTech’s Gama Abacus Centre in Perambra (Kozhikode) is a certified branch, providing Gama Abacus mental math classes for children. The curriculum builds a strong foundation in mathematics, cognitive skills, and confidence, with students progressing from abacus to advanced mental arithmetic.

  • 8-level curriculum over 24 months
  • Develops fast calculation, memory, and focus
  • Students participate in national competitions
